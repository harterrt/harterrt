* When
  * When you need a topline

* Costs
  * High startup cost

    * Need to have ETL in place
    * Someone needs to have intuition before publishing
* Other solutions
  * A report - good for initial investigations
    * Faster iteration
    * Encapsulates data at a given time - dashboards are ephemeral
